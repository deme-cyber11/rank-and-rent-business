Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get an estimate?

We typically respond to estimate requests within 24 hours and can often schedule a site visit within 2-3 business days. During your free consultation, we'll provide a detailed written estimate before you make any commitment.

Do you offer free estimates?

Yes! All estimates are completely free with no obligation. We'll visit your property, discuss your project, and provide a detailed written quote at no charge.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Orlando metropolitan area, including Orlando, Winter Park, Maitland, Altamonte Springs, Lake Mary, Oviedo, and surrounding communities. If you're unsure whether we serve your area, just give us a call!

How long does a typical driveway project take?

Most residential driveway projects take 3-5 days from start to finish, depending on size and complexity. This includes removal (if replacing), site preparation, installation, and curing time. We'll provide a specific timeline during your estimate.

Do you handle permits?

Yes, we handle all necessary permits and ensure compliance with local building codes. Permit requirements and fees are included in your estimate.
