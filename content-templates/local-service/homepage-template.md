## Content Guidelines

**Tone**: Professional, trustworthy, helpful, locally-focused

**Word Count Target**: 1,000-1,500 words (including all sections)

**Keyword Usage**:
- Primary keyword in H1, first paragraph, and 2-3 times naturally throughout
- Include city name 8-12 times naturally
- Use semantic variations of services

**Local SEO**:
- Mention specific neighborhoods
- Reference local landmarks (where natural)
- Emphasize local presence and fast response

**Call-to-Action Frequency**: Phone number should appear 4-6 times on page
